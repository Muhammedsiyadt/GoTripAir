Armenia Tour Packages - Embark on an enriching 03 Nights/04 Days journey to the cultural gem of the Caucasus - Armenia. Discover the historical wonders of Yerevan, visit the iconic ARARAT Brandy Factory, and immerse yourself in the art of Armenian carpet making at the Megerian Carpet factory. Itinerary:

DAY 1

Yerevan Arrival - City Tour & Cultural Treasures

Arrive in Yerevan and kickstart your Armenia tour with an exciting city tour. Visit the renowned ARARAT Brandy Factory for a taste of authentic Armenian cognac, explore the ancient manuscripts at the Matenadaran Museum, and immerse yourself in the art of Armenian carpet making at the Megerian Carpet Factory Museum.

DAY 2

Tsaghkadzor & Lake Sevan

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Tsaghkadzor with a scenic cable car ride to Mount Teghenis, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes. Next, experience the serene allure of Lake Sevan and witness the majestic Sevanavank Monastery perched on its shores.

DAY 3

Garni & Geghard - Unveiling Armenia's Heritage

Step back in time as you explore the ancient pagan temple of Garni, dedicated to the God of Sun Mihr. Continue your journey with a visit to the awe-inspiring Geghard Monastery, a UNESCO World Heritage site, intricately carved into the rock.

DAY 4

Farewell Yerevan

Bid farewell to the enchanting city of Yerevan as you check out of your hotel and transfer to Yerevan Airport. This marks the end of your unforgettable adventure in Armenia, filled with cultural treasures and natural wonders.

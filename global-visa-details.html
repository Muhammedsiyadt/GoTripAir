Apply Oman Visit Visa Online

Oman is a country situated on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula in Southwest Asia. It shares borders with the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, and Yemen.

Oman is known for its stunning landscapes, including desert dunes, rugged mountains, and pristine coastline along the Arabian Sea. The country’s rich history, vibrant culture, and welcoming hospitality attract travelers from around the world.

Oman is also a modern and progressive nation, with a diverse economy based on oil production, tourism, and commerce.

Oman Visit Visa Cost

The cost of an Oman visa varies depending on the type and duration of the visa. For example, a 10-day Oman visit visa may cost around 175 AED, while the prices for 30 days, 60 days, and 90 days visas are subject to change. It’s essential to check the latest visa fees before applying.

Understanding Oman Visit Visa Types

Oman offers various types of visit visas to accommodate different travel durations and purposes:

  • 10 Days Oman Visit Visa: Perfect for short trips or visa change.
  • 30 Days Oman Visit Visa: Ideal for a month-long vacation or business trip.
  • 60 Days Oman Visit Visa: Suitable for longer stays, allowing ample time for exploration.
  • 90 Days Oman Visit Visa: For extended visits or family reunions, providing flexibility and convenience.

Processing Time for Oman Visit Visa

Processing time for Oman visit visas typically takes around 3 days. However, it’s important to note that Oman’s immigration authorities maintain strict scrutiny over visit visas, prioritizing security measures. Applicants should ensure all documentation is accurate and complete to expedite the visa process and comply with immigration regulations.

Required Documents

To apply for an Oman visit visa, you’ll need the following documents:

  • Passport copy of the front and back pages, with a minimum validity of 6 months.
  • Color passport-sized photo, adhering to visa photo specifications.
